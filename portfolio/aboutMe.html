A little more about me

Who Am I?

I'm Jesús Zerpa, a UX/UI designer with a strong passion for art and technology, based in Buenos Aires, Argentina. Since I was young, I've been self-taught in art, learning to draw and create through YouTube videos. This passion for crafting intuitive and visually appealing user experiences, along with my constant desire to learn in an ever-expanding field, defines who I am both as a professional and a person.

My Compass: Values and Philosophy

My design approach is grounded in fundamental values like responsibility and respect, both towards others and myself. These values guide me in every project, ensuring that I set clear boundaries and maintain high standards in my work. I firmly believe in the importance of empathizing with users and understanding their needs to create effective, user-centered solutions.

The Art of Creating: My Artistic Side

Art has been a fundamental part of my life from a young age. Over the years, I've developed solid experience with Photoshop, a tool that has been integral to both my creative process in digital drawing and the creation of tattoo designs. During my time as a tattoo artist, I gained a unique perspective on design and attention to detail. These skills now enrich my ability to create visuals that are both appealing and functional in my UX/UI design work.

Diving into Creativity

One of my favorite parts of the design process is creating wireframes and prototypes, as well as video editing. I could spend days immersed in this phase, where I feel the structure and functionality of an interface truly take shape. The combination of my passion for art and my dedication to design allows me to develop creative and effective solutions that resonate with users.

The Zen of Stress: My Secret Energy

When it comes to handling stress, I view it as a positive motivator that pushes me to overcome challenges. However, when I need to unwind, I find the gym to be an effective way to stay balanced and focused on my projects. This practice helps me maintain a clear and productive mindset in my work.

Knowledge Explorer: Growing Nonstop

My motivation to keep learning in the field of technology comes from recognizing that it's a constantly expanding universe, full of new skills to acquire and concepts to explore. This drives me to continue growing in both UX/UI design and web development, always seeking opportunities to improve and add value.

What’s Next? Connect with Me!

As a UX/UI designer, I consider myself a creative and empathetic professional with a deep passion for art and technology. I value responsibility and respect in my work, allowing me to maintain an ethical and committed approach to everything I do. I’m motivated by the limitless potential of the tech field, always looking for ways to expand my knowledge and skills with the goal of creating impactful and meaningful digital experiences. Don’t hesitate to reach out and explore new opportunities together!
